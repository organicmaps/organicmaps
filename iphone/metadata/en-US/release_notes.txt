## Map

- NEW! Proper map wrapping at the antimeridian (±180° longitude) when dragging and rotating
- OpenStreetMap data updated as of April 4
- Wikipedia data updated as of April 1
- Added icons for food courts and city gates
- Added clothing shop subcategories: women, men, children, wedding, sports, underwear, plus office types
- Fixed Sydney subway line colors
- Fixed opening hours display for places with sunrise/sunset offsets

## Routing and Navigation

- NEW! Tap a cycling or hiking route to select it and view its elevation chart
- Improved multi-color rendering for hiking and cycling routes
- Cycling navigation now uses compass heading at low speeds
- Fixed a system error when planning routes near some regional borders
- You can now start a valid route even when prompted to download additional maps

## Bookmarks and Tracks

- NEW! Change all bookmark and track colors in a category at once
- Adjusted elevation gain and loss approximation on track graphs to better match other apps
- Improved display of HTML content with tables and images in bookmark details
- Added vertical separators for multi-segment tracks in the elevation chart
- Fixed bookmark/track list search when tapping it from Place Details
- Fixed point selection in the elevation chart for interpolated points
- Fixed GPS track recording dropping points between segments
- Fixed GeoJSON import
- Fixed a crash when exporting tracks

## Search

- Better search in the USA, with two million additional addresses parsed from TIGER Census data
- Updated search categories: added "Bread" for bakeries, "Shawarma" cuisine, and other synonym improvements
- Improved house-number address search
- Added US street synonyms: highway, hwy, freeway, fwy
- Fixed search results when city and state names are the same

## Editor / OpenStreetMap

- You can now add a new place to OpenStreetMap after selecting your current position on the map
- Fixed editor crosshair misalignment when pressing "Add Place"
- Increased the touch area of buttons in the "Add Place" navigation bar

## UI and UX

- Improved modal gestures: snap to the nearest step, close on edge pan, and restore the previous state when reopened
- Fixed daylight saving time handling for time zones

## Stability and Localization

- Improved stability and performance, reduced battery usage
- Updated translations

## Platform Support

- Due to changes in TestFlight and the App Store, this release supports only iOS 15 and later. iOS 12, 13, and 14 are no longer supported.
