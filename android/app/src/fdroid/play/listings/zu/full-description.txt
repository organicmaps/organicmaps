Organic Maps iyithuluzi elishesha nelinemininingwane elisebenza ngaphandle kokuxhumeka kwe-intanethi futhi line-GPS yokuhola indlela, elenzelwe ukuhamba, ukuhamba ngezintaba, ukuhamba ngezinyawo, kanye nokugibela ibhayisikili. Hlela, uthumele, futhi urekhoda izindlela, futhi ujabulele ukuqondiswa okuhle ngaphandle kokudla ibhethri lakho noma ukusebenzisa uxhumano lwe-intanethi. Hlola izindawo futhi uqondise ngokuphepha ngesikhathi sokuzijabulisa kwakho ngaphandle kanye nohambo lwakho emhlabeni wonke. Lolu hlelo lokusebenza lunikeza ukulanda kwemephu okungenamkhawulo kanye nezibuyekezo eziphindwa kabili ngenyanga. Lusekelwe ku-OpenStreetMap, lwenziwe ngabavolontiya, lugxile ekuvikelweni kwemfihlo, lusebenza ngaphandle kwe-intanethi ngokuphelele, futhi luyamahhala ngokuphelele.

<b>NGAPHANDLE KWENETHIWEKI:</b>

Lolu hlelo lokusebenza lusebenza ngokuphelele ngaphandle kwe-intanethi — kuhlanganisa ukusesha nokuhamba. Ungahlola imephu futhi uhambe ngaphandle kokuxhumeka ku-intanethi, ngaleyo ndlela ugcine idatha yeselula nemali yokuhamba phesheya. Futhi ngenxa yokungadluliseli idatha ngemuva kwesikrini, ibhethri lakho lihlala isikhathi eside kakhulu.

<b>SHESHA:</b>

Ukuzula, ukusesha, nokubala izindlela kusebenza ngokushesha okubonakalayo.

<b>OKUGCWELE IMINININGWANE NOKUVUZA:</b>

Amamephu agcwele imininingwane futhi avuselelwa kabili ngenyanga ukuze abonise izimo zangempela zomhlaba. Ngokungafani nabancintisana nabo abaningi abaziwayo, afaka izilabelama zokwakha, amabhentshi, izindlela zokuhamba ngezinyawo, nolunye ulwazi oluwusizo.

<b>ESEKELWE KU-OPENSTREETMAP:</b>

I-OpenStreetMap iyimephu yomhlaba yesitayela se-wiki eyakhiwe futhi igcinwe yizigidi zabavolontiya — abantu bendawo abazi kahle izindawo zabo. Lomzamo womphakathi uholela kumamephu anemininingwane eningi, kaningi anembile kakhulu kunalawo ezohwebo ezindaweni ezingakaze zidwetshwe kakhulu. I-Organic Maps ivuselela idatha yayo evela ku-OpenStreetMap njalo ngemva kwamaviki amabili, igcina amamephu akho esesikhathini futhi ethembekile. Futhi uma ubona ukuthi kukhona okushodayo, ungakwengeza ku-OpenStreetMap futhi usize ukuthuthukisa imephu kubo bonke.

<b>IMFIHLO KUZA KUQALA:</b>

Asiqoqi idatha noma izazisi. Lokhu kusho ukuthi akukho ngozi yokuvuza kwedatha futhi akukho ukuphazamiseka okufana nezikhangiso noma izaziso ezinesasaza — isipiliyoni semephu esihlanzekile, esizimele.

<b>IZICI EZIBALULEKILE</b>

<b>HLOLA IZINDAWO:</b>

Sesha ngokwekheli, igama, noma isigaba, thola izigidi zama-POI, futhi ubone imininingwane eningiliziwe efana namahora okuvula, izihloko ze-Wikipedia, ukufinyeleleka kwesihlalo esinesondo, nezinye imininingwane ewusizo.

<b>UKUZULAZULA NGE-GPS:</b>

Jabulela imiyalelo eqondiswa ngamazwi, okujika ngokujika. Dala imizila yokuhamba ngezinyawo, yokuhamba entabeni, yokugibela ibhayisikili, noma yokushayela — kuhlanganisa nemizila phakathi kwamadolobha, amazwe, nezindawo eziningi zokuma. Thola izindlela ezingcono kakhulu zohambo lwakho lwe-metro nokuhamba kwakho kwansuku zonke. I-Android Auto iyasekelwa.

<b>IZINDLELA ZOKUHAMBA NGEZINYAWO NANGEBHAYISIKILI EMHLABENI WONKE:</b>

Vumela izendlalelo zemephu ezikhethekile ukuze uthole izindlela ezidume kakhulu zokuhamba ngezinyawo nokugibela ibhayisikili emhlabeni wonke, zonke zithathwe ku-OpenStreetMap. Shintsha phakathi kwezendlalelo ukuze ubone imigqa enemibala yamabhayisikili ne-MTB, kanye nemizila esemthethweni yokuhamba ngezinyawo.

<b>EZOKUTHUTHA ZOMPHAKATHI:</b>

Bona izinombolo zemigqa yezokuthutha zomphakathi eziteshini. Uhlelo lokuhamba bukhoma nezindlela okwamanje kusathuthukiswa.

<b>IZITAYELA ZEMEPHU:</b>

Khetha izendlalelo zemephu ezifanele umsebenzi wakho — Ngaphandle, Imigqa Yemibimbi, Ukuhamba Ngezinyawo, Ukugibela Ibhayisikili, noma iMetro/Isitimela Esingaphansi Komhlaba.

<b>UMREKHODI WOMZILA:</b>

Rekhoda, ngena, thumela ngaphandle, futhi wabelane ngezindlela zakho nezindawo ozithandayo. Isekela amafomethi e-KML, KMZ, GPX, ne-GeoJSON.
